Test the effect of two label elements on a single input

Published: June 6, 2019

Two label elements for one input

results

Tested with latest versions of screen readers and browsers available at time of publish.

  • iOS VO & Safari only announces first label as accessible name.
  • macOS VO & Safari only announces first label as accessible name.
  • macOS VO & Chrome announces both labels as accessible name.
  • TalkBack & Firefox announces both labels as accessible name (no space between words).
  • TalkBack & Chrome announces both labels as accessible name.
  • JAWS & Chrome announces both labels as accessible name.
  • JAWS & Firefox announces both labels as accessible name.
  • JAWS & IE11 only announces the second label as accessible name.
  • JAWS & pre-Chromium Edge only announces the second label as accessible name.
  • JAWS & Chromium Edge announces both labels as accessible name.
  • NVDA & Chrome announces both labels as accessible name.
  • NVDA & Firefox announces both labels as accessible name.
  • NVDA & IE11 only announces the second label as accessible name.
  • NVDA & pre-Chromium Edge only announces the second label as accessible name.
  • NVDA & Chromium Edge announces both labels as accessible name.
